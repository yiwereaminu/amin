Bio

Aminu Yiwere is a versatile and skilled professional with a strong foundation in computer engineering, specializing in data analysis, network support, and frontend development. As a recent graduate, he brings a fresh perspective and a passion for continuous learning to the field of technology.

Currently serving as a data analyst at Ernest Chemists Ltd, Aminu collaborates with the internal audit team, employing his expertise in data analytics and client communication to contribute to the success of the organization.

Throughout his academic journey at the University of Ghana, Aminu demonstrated exceptional dedication and innovation. As part of a talented team, he contributed to the development of a diagnostic tool using deep learning techniques, revolutionizing the identification of rheumatic disorders in patients. Moreover, he implemented blockchain technology to ensure secure data storage across multiple nodes, showcasing his forward-thinking approach to problem-solving.

Aminu Yiwere's commitment to excellence extends beyond his professional endeavors. Recognized for his academic achievements, he was honored with the prestigious Ghana National Petroleum Commission (GNPC) Scholarship, further fueling his passion for making a positive impact through technology.

Looking ahead, Aminu aspires to leverage his skills and knowledge to excel in challenging and dynamic work environments, fostering innovation and driving positive change in the world of computer engineering.
